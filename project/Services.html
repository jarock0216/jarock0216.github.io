Our Grooming Packages

At Darryah's Doggy Dews, we offer a variety of grooming services designed to meet your dog's specific needs, ensuring they look and feel their best.

Basic Grooming

This package includes:

  • Bath with all-natural shampoo
  • Brushing and blow-dry
  • Nail trimming
  • Ear cleaning
  • Basic sanitary trim

Price: $50 - $80 (depending on breed and coat condition)

Deluxe Grooming

Includes everything in Basic Grooming plus:

  • Full-body haircut and styling
  • Teeth brushing
  • Special coat conditioning treatment

Price: $90 - $120 (depending on breed and coat condition)

Pampered Pet Package

The ultimate spa day for your dog, featuring:

  • Everything in Deluxe Grooming
  • Luxury paw treatment (paw soak and pad massage)
  • Facial scrub
  • Specialized coat treatments (e.g., de-shedding, whitening)

Price: $130 - $180 (depending on breed and coat condition)

Additional Services

These add-ons can be included with any grooming package:

  • Teeth Cleaning: $15
  • De-Shedding Treatment: $25
  • Flea & Tick Treatment: $20
  • Hand Stripping: $50 (for wire-haired breeds)
