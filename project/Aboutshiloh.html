Meet Shiloh

Shiloh is a lovable Shi-Poo, a Shih Tzu and Poodle mix. With his curly, fluffy fur and calm demeanor, he’s known for being the most chill dog around. He’s always sweet and enjoys spending time with his favorite humans.

Shiloh gives affectionate kisses when he’s in the mood, especially after a good cuddle or play session. His relaxed nature makes him a joy to be around.

Personality Traits

  • Temperament: Chill, sweet, and laid-back
  • Fur: Curly and fluffy, perfect for snuggles
  • Favorite Activities: Lounging around, giving kisses, and occasional playtime
  • Special Traits: Extremely cooperative and smart, knows when it’s playtime and when to relax
