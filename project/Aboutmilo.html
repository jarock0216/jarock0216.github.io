Meet Milo

Milo is a small, adorable Shi-Chi (a Shih Tzu and Chihuahua mix) known for his playful energy and sweet nature. At just a few pounds, he’s compact, but his personality fills the room!

Milo has straight, soft fur that makes him perfect for cuddles, and he loves to give lots of kisses any chance he get to anyone who shows him attention.

Despite his small size, Milo is full of energy and loves running around, playing fetch, and exploring new places.

Though he’s always energetic, he enjoys curling up on soft blankets, or sitting in laps for some quality snuggle time.

Personality Traits

  • Size: Small but mighty
  • Temperament: Friendly, outgoing, and full of kisses
  • Energy Level: Very energetic, loves playing fetch
  • Favorite Activities: Exploring, cuddling, and giving kisses
  • Special Traits: Straight-haired and loves being the center of attention
